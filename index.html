Hi, We're Luxloop.

We are a creative technology studio, focused on finding new ways to captivate, inspire and interact with our fellow humans.

We are a hybrid agency/production company forged in the fires of commercial and documentary film-production combined with an unceasing impulse to experiment and explore new and near-future formats.

If you’re interested in creating high-impact, interactive experiences that challenge what you believe is possible, let’s collaborate!

What We Do

Interactive Development and Production
We create original content for all platforms, ranging from web and mobile apps to completely unique spatial installations or custom hardware. We have created app-based non-linear documentaries, interactive art gallery installations, computational design for print media, interactive design for live theatre, and practical visual effects for films and music videos.

Creative Technology Consultants
In addition to producing original material, we love helping others bring their ideas to new platforms. We can help manage a project through every stage and set best-practices throughout the whole production process or be brought on to a project as part of the post-production workflow.
