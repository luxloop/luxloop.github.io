Cloud Control

2015 — Mobile-Controlled Visual Lighting Effects

Client: Riot Time Films

Our friends at Riot Time reached out to us when they needed some help creating a key element for their new short film, People are Turning Into Clouds. The story follows a couple, John and Elenor, who must discover why Eleanor keeps turning into a cloud.

The cloud is a key character in the film, and Riot Time wanted to do as much as possible with practical effects instead of CG. To meet the challenge of creating a realistic cloud that could show emotions through lightning, Luxloop created a custom programmable light system for the cloud that could be triggered wirelessly via an application on a mobile phone.

Creative Direction by Mandy Mandelstein
Software by Luxloop
