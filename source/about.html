What We Do

We started by thinking about new ways that stories could live in the world.

Technology is allowing people to interface with story in new spaces and in new ways. We are excited by these new oportunities to foster interaction and play in ways that people have never seen before. What does it mean that 86% of adults now carry a super computer in their pocket? How can we take advantage of this under-utilized computing power? What if we can use technology to re-think the concept of the theater?

We dream, sketch, prototype, and play.
We build apps to tell stories.
We use projection to transform worlds.
We like to find ways to make magic real.
