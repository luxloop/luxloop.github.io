Black Cloak
Immersive Environment for Pop-up Dinner by Michelin-Star Chef

Luxloop was approached to design and execute the projection-mapped environment for a 36-seat pop-up dinner during Art Basel Miami by Michelin Star chef Vicky Cheng and renowned pastry chef Andrés Lara.

Tasked with creating a unique environment for each of the five courses, the aim was to transport the guests to another world as each dish was served.

We created original visual content based on the themes of each dish, which were ultimately composited and controlled live, responding to the movements and interactions of the guests, staff, and performers.
