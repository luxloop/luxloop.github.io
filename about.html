What We Do

We tell stories using new and near-future technology to create engaging interactive experiences that evoke wonder and foster a sense of discovery for artists and brands alike.

We often think about how technology can change the world around us. Rather than focus on a specific technology, we focus on story and engagement and pick the technology that will allow us to bring the idea to life.

We dream, sketch, prototype, and play.
We build apps to tell stories.
We use projection to transform worlds.
We like to find ways to make magic real.
