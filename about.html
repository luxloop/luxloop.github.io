Hi, We're Luxloop.

We are a creative technology studio, focused on finding new ways to captivate, inspire and interact with our fellow humans.

We are a hybrid agency/production company born in the fires of commercial and documentary film-production combined with an unceasing impulse to experiment and explore new and near-future formats. We are a collective of provocateurs, inventors, artists, programmers, filmmakers, hackers, and dreamers excited about the potential of media to foment play and discovery. We create experiential tools to empower creatives and brands looking for new, memorable ways to connect with audiences.

Not only do we fill in the dots, but we help other cutting-edge storytellers refine and elaborate on their ideas, as we generate the media and code to bring them to life.

If you’re interested in creating high-impact, interactive experiences that challenge what you believe is possible, let’s collaborate!

Collaborators

After working together in traditional film media, Ivaylo Getov and Mandy Mandelstein discovered their mutal fascination with new technology and founded luxloop. Now their dreaming of ways to make the world more magical, by making the world a bit more interactive and interesting.

Ivaylo Getov
As a cinematographer, Ivaylo's work has been featured on PBS POV, Vice, MTV and has shown at festivals around the country including DocNYC and the Brooklyn International Film Festival. In his search for new forms of image-making, Ivaylo discovered creative coding and dove in headfirst. As an interactive developer and designer, his work has been shown and experienced at Tribeca Interactive Playground, 3LD Art and Technology Center, Geneva Lift Conference, and The New School. In his free time, Ivaylo can be found working on weird video art and speaking with small machines in binary code.

Mandy Mandelstein
Mandy Mandelstein is a director, cinematographer, and visual interactive artist living and working in New York City. She originally hails from Ausitn, Tx where she was raised on a healthy dose of punk rock and Andy Warhol. Her directorial music video work has been written up by SPIN, MTV, Stereogum, and Filter Magazine. She’s also worked in production on many commercials for clients such as MTV, Apple, Verizon, and Burberry, as well as on independent films, including Deep Powder (TFF 2013), Teenage (TFF 2013), Manglehorn (Venice, SXSW).
