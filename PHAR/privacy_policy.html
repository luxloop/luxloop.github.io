After ALife Ahead Privacy Policy and End User License Agreement

This policy was last updated on June 4th,2017.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application After ALife Ahead(“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Luxloop LLC. The Application is an augmented reality application to accompany the installation by Pierre Huyghe during Skulptur Projekte Munster 2017. 

WHAT DATA DO WE COLLECT?

Location Services and Camera Sensor data is enabled on the user’s mobile device to enable and move the camera for the Augmented Reality features.

The Application may access certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, your mobile operating system. This data is used to improve the Augmented Reality experience, and is not stored or transmitted. This data and any related personal information is not collected, stored, shared, transmitted, or disclosed by Luxloop LLC. Luxloop LLC does not collect additional unique identifiers or personal information using the After A Life Ahead application.

The After ALife Ahead is an application is built using the Unity game engine. The automatic collection of unique device identifiers and hardware statistics by the Unity game engine has been disabled. You can access the Unity privacy policy here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

HOW DOES THE APPLICATION COLLECT PRECISE REAL TIME LOCATION INFORMATION OF THE DEVICE AND HOW IS IT USED?


When you visit the mobile application, we use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current location within the space to enable the spatial camera capabilities of the Augmented Reality functions in this application. Location information is only used in order to determine your position within the exhibition. Location data is not used if you are not at the location of the exhibition. We do not store, share or disclose your current location or location information with other users or partners.

If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should delete the mobile application from your phone as per standard app removal procedures. The Augmented Reality feature of this application will not work without access to precise Location Information.

PRIVACY FAQ

How does the After ALife Aheads app work?

The After ALife Aheads app utilizes augmented reality technology to display the virtual content of the exhibition entitled After ALife Ahead. This process is done on the device and does not transmit images to a cloud based service or 3rd party.

What information does the After ALife Aheads app collect?

A detailed list of all the information collected can be found in the app privacy policy above. The information collected includes the device IP address. The IP address is used internally by Vuforia (PTC Inc.) to determine the country / region of use.

Does the After ALife Aheads app use in-app purchasing?

No, the After ALife Ahead app does not include any forms of in-app purchasing.

Do you have social media links in the After ALife Aheads app?

No, the After A Life Ahead app does not include any form of social media links or other social media integration.

Does your app include advertising?

The After ALife Aheads application does not include banner ads or targeted advertising.
